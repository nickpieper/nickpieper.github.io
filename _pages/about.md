Nick is a motivated cybersecurity professional located in Buffalo, New York with multiple years of hands-on experience in technology. He has managed a corporation’s Splunk platform and built security-related reports and alerts to aid in detection.

With an eye towards future technology and securing it, Nick has researched, built, and introduced apps that he believes would improve the security posture of the organization. These tools fall under the realm of cyber threat intelligence, SIEMs, and security testing. The successful deployment of an internal phishing program with over 1000 recipients companywide every quarter solidifies the fact that he can get the job done.

When it comes to cyber threats, Nick keeps on top of the news with a variety of personal resources including RSS feeds, news aggregators, and personal contacts. He is a member of a cyber threat-sharing group that actively exchanges information in order to improve the cybersecurity of our global businesses and contacts.

Additionally, Nick is currently studying for a Network Operations and Security degree (B.S.) at WGU and plans to graduate in 2021/2022. He currently holds certs from Cisco, Linux Professional Institute, CompTIA, and AWS.
